    "The gamma function is defined as\n",
    "\n",
    "\\begin{equation}\n",
    "\\Gamma(x) \\equiv \\int_{0}^{\\infty} u^{x-1}e^{-u} \\,du \n",
    "\\end{equation}\n",
    "\n",
    "There are three important facts about the gamma function, with relatively short justifications\n",
    "\n",
    "**The recursive identity**\n",
    "\n",
    "\\begin{equation}\n",
    "\\Gamma(x+1) = x \\Gamma(x)\n",
    "\\end{equation}\n",
    "\n",
    "*Proof.* Integrate by parts.\n",
    "\n",
    "\\begin{align*}\n",
    "\\Gamma(x+1) &= \\int_{0}^{\\infty} u^x e^{-u} \\,du\\\\\n",
    "&= \\left[-e^{-u}u^x\\right]_{0}^{\\infty} - \\int_{0}^{\\infty} xu^{x-1}e^{-u} \\,du \\\\\n",
    "&= \\left[0 + 1\\cdot 0\\right] -x \\int_{0}^{\\infty} u^{x-1}e^{-u} \\,du = x\\Gamma(x)\n",
    "\\end{align*}\n"
   ]
  },
  {
   "cell_type": "markdown",
